Learn about common scams and how to protect yourself

üöî Digital Arrest Scams

What it is: Scammers pose as police, CBI, or government officials claiming you're involved in illegal activity.

Red flags: Urgent demands for money, threats of arrest, asks for remote access to devices.

Protection: Hang up immediately. Real police never demand money over phone. Call 1930 to report.

üí≥ UPI & Payment Scams

What it is: Fake payment apps or websites that steal your money and banking details.

Red flags: Apps with similar names (PayTM vs Paytm), screen overlays during payments.

Protection: Only download apps from Play Store. Verify package names. Never share UPI PIN.

üì± OTP Scams

What it is: Scammers trick you into sharing One-Time Passwords (OTPs) to access your accounts.

Red flags: Calls asking for OTP, "verify account" messages, urgent payment requests.

Protection: NEVER share OTP with anyone. Banks never ask for OTP over phone.

üèõÔ∏è Government Impersonation

What it is: Scammers pretend to be from IT Department, Tax Office, or other agencies.

Red flags: Threats of legal action, demands for immediate payment, suspicious caller IDs.

Protection: Government never asks for payment over phone. Verify via official websites.

üìû Phishing Calls & SMS

What it is: Fake calls/messages claiming your account is blocked, you won a prize, etc.

Red flags: Creates urgency, asks for personal info, suspicious links in SMS.

Protection: Don't click links in SMS. Call official customer care numbers to verify.
